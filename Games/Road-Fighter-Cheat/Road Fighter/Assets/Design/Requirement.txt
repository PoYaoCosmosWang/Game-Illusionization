In this game:
1. a game manager will control the flow of the game
2. game manager will produce cubes, including target and intervene
3. game manager will produce obstacles
4. player can move up and down
5. when player touch target cube, the score will increase.
6. when player touch intervene cube, the score will decrease.
	once the score is smaller than zero, player lose the game
7. the type of cube could be more than which mentioned above.
8. cube with "?" can change the color of player.
9. player bump into the obstacle will decrease score with correponding demage
10. the cubes can move straight or go up and down
11. the cubes can have pattern
